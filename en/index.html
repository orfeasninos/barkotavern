Our Story

Barko Tavern is a family-run taverna in Adamas, created to offer the classic Greek holiday experience: warm hospitality, homemade food and a relaxed island vibe. Since 1993, we have been keeping the traditional recipes of Milos alive, using simple ingredients and plenty of love. Here you will enjoy dishes that truly taste like Greece — slow-cooked meals, grilled specialties and shared plates. Ideal for families, friends and couples looking for an authentic dining experience in Milos.
