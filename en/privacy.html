Privacy Policy

Barko Tavern respects the privacy of visitors to its website. Below we explain in simple terms what data may be collected, for what purpose, and how you can control your choices.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Barko Tavern, Adamas, Milos, Greece.

For privacy matters you may contact us at barkomilos@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

The website may use Google Analytics for anonymous statistics (e.g. device type, general country/region, time spent and pages viewed).

We do not collect personal data such as name, phone number or email unless you choose to contact us directly.

3. Purpose of Processing

Data (where applicable) is used exclusively for statistical analysis and improvement of user experience and website performance.

4. Cookies

The website uses analytics cookies (Google Analytics). These cookies are activated only after explicit consent via the cookie banner.

You may accept or reject cookies and change your choice at any time.

5. Legal Basis of Processing

For analytics cookies, the legal basis is your consent. You may withdraw it at any time.

6. Data Recipients

For anonymous statistics we use Google Analytics. Google acts as a service provider/processor on our behalf.

7. Transfers Outside the EU/EEA

Third-party services (such as Google) may involve data transfers outside the European Union/EEA.

8. Data Retention

Statistical data is retained for the period necessary for website improvement and according to Google Analytics settings.

9. Your Rights

You have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and data portability (where applicable).

You may also lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority.

10. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page.

Summary

  • We use analytics only to improve the website
  • Analytics cookies are activated only after consent
  • You can change your choice at any time
