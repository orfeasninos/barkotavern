4. Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a website, so it can remember your preferences. They do not have access to files or documents on your device.

This website uses the following cookies:

  • cookie_consent (Necessary) — Stores your cookie choice. Duration: 1 year.
  • _ga, _ga_* (Analytics — Google Analytics) — Distinguishes anonymous users for statistics. Duration: up to 2 years. Activated only after acceptance.
  • Google Maps cookies (Third-party — e.g. NID, SIDCC) — Set automatically by the Google Maps iframe on the home page and reservations page when the page loads. Duration: as per Google's policy.

Umami does not use cookies.

5. Purpose & Legal Basis for Processing

  • Google Analytics: Improving the website through anonymous statistics. Legal basis: Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).
  • Umami Analytics: Anonymous aggregated statistics without cookies and without the ability to identify individuals. Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Google Maps (Embed): Displaying the location for visitors' convenience. Google may process your IP address when the map loads. Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — finding directions to the tavern is a reasonable interest of both visitor and business.
  • Contact (email/phone): Responding to your enquiries. Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

We do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling (Article 22 GDPR).

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access (Article 15): to find out what data we process about you.
  • Rectification (Article 16): correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure (Article 17): the "right to be forgotten".
  • Restriction of processing (Article 18).
  • Objection (Article 21): to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Portability (Article 20): to receive your data in a structured format.
  • Withdrawal of consent: at any time via cookie settings or by contacting us.

To exercise any right, please send a request to barkotavernmilos@gmail.com. We respond within 1 month of receipt (Article 12 GDPR).

9. Data Breach

We take all appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. In the event of a breach that may endanger your rights, we will notify the HDPA within 72 hours (Article 33 GDPR) and, where required, inform you directly (Article 34 GDPR).

10. Minors

This website is not directed at minors under the age of 15 (the threshold set by Greek Law 4624/2019). If you are under 15, please use this website only with the consent of a parent or guardian.
