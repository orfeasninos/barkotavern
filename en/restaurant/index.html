History & Tradition

Barko Tavern is a family-run taverna in Adamas, created to give you the “classic” Greek holiday experience in Milos: warm hospitality, homemade flavors, and a relaxed atmosphere. Since 1993, we’ve been keeping the island’s traditional recipes alive with simple ingredients and lots of love.

Here you’ll taste dishes that feel truly Greek — slow-cooked pots, grilled favorites, and shared meze plates. Perfect for families, groups of friends, and couples looking for an authentic, honest dinner in Milos.

Location & Atmosphere

You’ll find us in Adamas, very close to the port — an ideal stop when you first arrive on the island or after a full day exploring beaches like Sarakiniko and Tsigrado. Easy to reach, no stress.

The atmosphere is what travelers love: a cool courtyard, summer shade, warm evening lights, and the feel of a truly authentic Greek taverna. Sit back, relax, and let Milos do the rest.

Cuisine & Specialties

Our cooking is purely Greek and traditional, with an island character. We cook daily and prefer simple recipes that highlight quality ingredients. You’ll find home-style dishes, grilled favorites, seafood, and meze plates that pair perfectly with ouzo or wine.

If you’re visiting Milos for the first time, don’t leave without tasting local specialties like pitarakia and skordolazana. Ask us about the “dish of the day” and fresh fish, depending on the season and the day’s catch.

Hospitality & Service

Hospitality is part of the Barko experience. We’ll welcome you with a smile, help you navigate the menu, and recommend dishes based on what you enjoy. Whether you’re here for a quick lunch or a relaxed dinner, you’ll feel at home.

Summer evenings on the island can get busy — so we recommend a reservation. And as in the best Greek tavernas, your meal often ends with a small complimentary treat.
