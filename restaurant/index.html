Traditional Taverna in Milos

The Restaurant

History & Tradition

We are a family-run taverna in Adamas, Milos, and we like to keep things simple. We cook the way we learned at home: with good ingredients, clean flavours and without rushing.

Since 1993, we have made sure that every dish carries the character of Greek cuisine and of Milos. Whether you come for a relaxed lunch or an evening out with friends, we want you to feel at ease — as if you had sat down at the table of old friends.

Location & Atmosphere

You will find us in Adamas, very close to the port and easily reachable from wherever you are on the island. It is a spot that works just as well for your first stop after the ferry as it does for a quiet dinner after an evening stroll.

Our space is cool and relaxed, with a summer courtyard and a peaceful setting. Come here to eat well, have a good conversation and enjoy your time — no fuss, no rush.

Cuisine & Specialties

In our kitchen, we care about flavour and quality — not excess. We cook every day with fresh ingredients and recipes we know well, so that every dish is balanced and just right.

You will find classic Greek flavours, local dishes from Milos and options that suit both a light lunch and a more substantial dinner. Our specialties change with the seasons and whatever the best produce happens to be at any given time.

Hospitality & Service

For us, hospitality is something simple and natural. We welcome you the way we would welcome friends: with a smile, a light touch and respect for your time.

Our team is always there when you need us — without pressure and without rushing you along. We want you to leave full, relaxed and feeling like you had a great time. That says everything, as far as we are concerned.
