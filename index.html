Ourstory

⏰ 13:00 – 00:00 daily

We are a family taverna in Adamas, Milos, and we like to keep things simple. We cook the way we learned at home: with quality ingredients, clean flavours and without rushing. Since 1993, we have made sure that every dish carries the character of Greek cuisine and of Milos. Whether you come for a relaxed lunch or an evening out with friends, we want you to feel at ease — as if you were sitting at a friend's table.
